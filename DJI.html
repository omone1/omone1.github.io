Your Dreams of Owning a Tank are Now Possible

Joseph Moran

6/13/2019

Remember the days of being a little kid and letting your imagination run wild. One day you were an astronaut the next you were a firefighter. Maybe even as a kid you imagined yourself with your very own taking driving around and blowing things up. Well owning your own tank is can now be more than just a dream. No its not a real tank that has the capabilities to blow up buildings but instead a new drone from DJI. The new RoboMaster S1 from dji is a drone tank that come with a built in camera for driving as well as a blaster for battles with friends if they happen to have one as well. The thing that makes this new drone interesting is that is was built for learning. Whether you are new to programming and software or new to drones the RoboMaster is perfect for beginners. This drone has 46 programable components and supports python as well as scratch. On top of this the RoboMaster has AI capabilities that further its ability to teach its users about applications in the software and robotics world. The applications of this drone are very interesting as it is a sort of smarter RC car. The possibilities are endless, maybe you want to have you track down and shoot your friends or put a grasping arm and program it to turn of lights around the house. Whether you want to use it for fun or practical purposes the RoboMaster S1 from DJI is a great drone for beginners and experts alike and c’mon who doesn’t want to control a tank.
